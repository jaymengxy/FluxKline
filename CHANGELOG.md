## 0.1.0

- Initial release
- K-line (candlestick) chart with pan, zoom, and crosshair gestures
- Technical indicators: MA, BOLL, MACD, KDJ, RSI, WR
- Sub-chart support (up to 2 simultaneous sub-charts)
- Real-time price line with auto-scroll
- Trade overlay (position & order lines)
- Mini K-line sparkline widget
- Trend chart widget
- Customizable theming via `FluxKlineTheme`
